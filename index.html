Product Development Journey

A structured, gate-driven NPD lifecycle that takes every product from concept evaluation through validation to approved mass production.

01

Concept Development

Requirement analysis, feasibility study, and prototype definition for new IT hardware & electronics SKUs.

02

EVT — Engineering Validation Test

First functional prototypes evaluated for engineering performance, design intent, and core functionality.

03

DVT — Design Validation Test

Design verified against specs, reliability, and manufacturability; corrective design improvements driven.

04

PVT — Production Validation Test

Pilot production runs validated for process stability, yield, and AQL batch acceptance.

05

Mass Production Approval

Final go/no-go decision and GTM handoff — 20+ SKUs approved with zero critical field escapes.
